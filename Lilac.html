Lilac

Description

Here is one of the models who is triumphing this year. Its purple color, the heart shape of its bracts, the lilac shades ... are truly amazing. Our Anthurium Andreanum Lila has a retro, elegant and sensual look. We chose this color because it conveys calm and tranquility, balancing the environment. Its best feature? The flowers last a long time and it is not a delicate plant.

Measurements: Height of about 30 - 35 cm from the roots and diameter of 12cm
Plants are living beings and this means that one will always be different from the other. Your plant will look very similar to the one you see in the photo, but it will have a unique and natural look.

Why is Lilac special?

  • It is a simple plant to care for.
  • It does not get along well with children or pets.
  • It will arrive inside a box designed specifically to protect it.

Property

Size approx

Humidity

It is a tropical plant, it will happily live between 15 and 20 ÂºC in a humid environment. She doesn't like sudden changes in temperature.

Level of care

Very simple to take care of. Ideal for mothers and fathers of plants who take their first steps in the plant world.

Irrigation

Moderate: Water it from spring to fall when you notice the earth is dry, the substrate should be moist but not waterlogged. In winter it needs less water.

Lighting

She likes to live in a bright place with no direct sun, for example near a sunny window. If it doesn't get enough light, it won't bloom.

Scientific name

Anthurium Andreanum
