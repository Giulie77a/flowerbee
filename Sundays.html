Description

Sundays is the perfect bouquet for those who want to enjoy the moment without thinking about tomorrow. A delicate combination of roses and limonium that mixes green and pink tones. One of our classics slightly reinvented to continue to have the well deserved success. When the bouquet arrives, gently remove the outer petals of the roses. We leave them to protect the heart of the flower during the journey. The green foliage of the bouquet may vary slightly from the photo.
