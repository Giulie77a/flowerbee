Our key points


✿ We work directly with farmers

We eliminate unnecessary intermediaries and work directly with farmers. We are committed to them for the long term and guarantee fair prices.


✿ We ship super fresh flowers and plants

We make flowers and plants look like they have just been picked.


✿ We create iconic designs

Nature, art and everyday life inspire us in the creation of iconic collections and designs capable of upsetting the routine. We like to do things well and with enthusiasm so that people welcome our flowers with joy.


✿ We do magic

Our revolution is also about changing the way people buy and receive flowers or plants at home. We create unforgettable experiences, the ones that everyone would like to recommend. All this is possible only thanks to a team like ours.
